What Is Yoga?

âœ¦ âœ¦ âœ¦

Yoga is an ancient practice of developing harmony between body, mind and spirit. It is an exploration of who we are as human beings. It teaches us to be mindful of our thoughts and emotions, and it helps us heal both physically and internally. To master yoga is to master yourself; to dwell firm in the present moment.

Aerial Yoga

Practice yoga suspended in silk hammocks, taking traditional poses into the air for a unique, gravity-defying experience. Aerial Yoga is artful & beautiful - and the hammock enables you to access difficult yoga asanas with more ease.

Vinyasa

Flow through sequences of poses synchronized with the breath, creating a dynamic and meditative practice that builds heat and focus. Vinyasa Yoga is all about fluidity; it rejuvenates the body and restores inner grace.

Hatha

Build a strong foundation with traditional Hatha Yoga - a practice that helps you find balance in a world of dualities. Hatha is an incredibly balanced and versatile yoga style, making it an ideal pick for beginners.

Kundalini

Awaken to your inner self with Kundalini Yoga - a practice that focuses on energy work and self-transformation. Kundalini is an incredibly deep yoga style, perfect for those who want to elevate their practice to a new level.
