Upcoming Events

We regularly offer themed workshops, transformative retreats, and comprehensive teacher trainings

Workshops

Our workshops are short but intensive sessions that explore specific aspects of yoga. We often blend physical exercise with breathwork, meditation, sound healing, and yogic philosophy - going beyond what's possible during a traditional class.

Retreats

To us, a retreat is a step back from the hectic reality of our daily lives. Our retreats are tailored to help you heal & transform, at the same time giving you space to rest and regain balance.

Teacher Trainings

The highest joy of a teacher is to see their students become wonderful yogis. Our teacher trainings give you all the tools you need to begin sharing the practice of yoga with the world. All our programmes come with an internationally-recognized Yoga Alliance certification and cover all the essential modules such as alignment, anatomy, philosophy, and more.
